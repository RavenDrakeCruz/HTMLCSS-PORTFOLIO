PLP LOGO ANIMATION

This project is a 3D animated representation of the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig (PLP) logo, created using Blender. The animation brings the university's emblem to life by integrating it into a cinematic scene. The centerpiece of the design is the PLP logo, positioned within a grand, sci-fi-inspired structure, symbolizing strength, innovation, and academic excellence.

Occupulse

OccuPulse is an innovative Machine Learning project powered by the K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm. Designed to assist students in enhancing their employability prospects, the platform leverages both an employability evaluation form and predictive analytics to offer valuable insights. Students can input their scores from the employability evaluation form, and the prediction system will automatically assess their employability, categorizing them as either 'Employable' or 'Less Employable'. During the training of our model, we achieved an impressive 91% accuracy, showcasing the system's effectiveness in predicting employability outcomes.
